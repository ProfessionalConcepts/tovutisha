The TOVUTISHA Legacy

Beyond the Digital Divide.

A Masinde Muliro University Outreach Initiative dedicated to empowering every school with a professional digital identity.

Institutional Origin

Our Foundation

Established as a flagship outreach initiative by Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology (MMUST), TOVUTISHA translates to "Website-ify".

We believe every Kenyan child deserves a school that is visible, transparent, and connected to the global economy.

The Strategic Gap

The Disparity

For too long, professional digital presence was a luxury reserved for high-end institutions. Public and rural schools faced prohibitive technical hurdles.

We shatter these barriers by providing institutional-grade websites for a sustainable flat fee of KES 5,000 per year.

The Tech Roadmap

National Vision

Aligned with KoTDA and the Ministry of Education, we are building the digital foundation for Kenya's future leaders.

We envision a Kenya where every school operates at the forefront of digital literacy and excellence.
