# PRD: TOVUTISHA - Affordable Kenyan School Websites ## Project Overview TOVUTISHA (Kiswahili for "Website-ify") is a MMUST outreach project providing affordable websites to Kenyan schools for a flat fee of KES 5,000 per year. The platform handles school applications, manual admin approval, and automated school website generation. ## Core Principles - **Affordability:** KES 5,000/year flat fee. - **Simplicity:** No online payments; manual offline billing and approval. - **Identity:** Mandatory backlinks to MMUST and KoTDA. - **Accessibility:** Searchable directory of Kenyan schools by county and type. ## Key Functional Requirements 1. **Public Application Flow:** Schools apply via a form (47 counties). 2. **Admin Review:** Super Admins approve/reject applications manually. 3. **Automated Site Generation:** Approval triggers a subdomain (`school.tovutisha.mmust.ac.ke`) and a standardized template. 4. **School Admin Portal:** Approved schools manage their news, events, and documents. 5. **Directory:** A public-facing searchable database of all incorporated schools. ## Technical Stack - **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI, Lucide Icons. - **Backend:** Node.js, Express, Prisma (PostgreSQL), JWT. - **Maps:** OpenStreetMap/Leaflet for school locations. ## NO-GO Zones - No M-Pesa or online payment integrations. - No automated invoicing. - No subscription management code. ## Visual Identity - **Primary Colors:** MMUST Blue/Gold or a professional educational palette (Navy & Teal). - **Typography:** Clean, sans-serif (Inter or Roboto). - **Components:** High-contrast, mobile-first, and accessible.
